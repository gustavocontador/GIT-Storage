# Task: Roundtable Decision

**ID:** roundtable-decision
**Tier:** All (Decision meeting with Roundtable + Human)
**Duration:** 4-6 hours
**Elicit:** false

## Objective

Conduct formal roundtable discussion where 3 strategists present findings, debate tradeoffs, and reach consensus on which #1 opportunity to pursue.

## What Happens

Structured decision-making meeting where:

1. **AI Strategist** presents technical opportunities
2. **Business Strategist** presents market viability
3. **Product Manager** presents user value
4. **Mentor** provides founder wisdom
5. **Master Coordinator** synthesizes discussion
6. **You** make final decision with full context

## Meeting Structure (4 hours)

### Hour 1: Data Presentation
- AI Strategist: 20 min presentation
- Business Strategist: 20 min presentation
- Product Manager: 20 min presentation

### Hour 2: Questions & Clarification
- You ask questions
- Challenge assumptions
- Request deeper analysis

### Hour 3: Debate & Discussion
- Strategists discuss tradeoffs
- Mentor adds founder perspective
- Data Specialist presents market metrics
- Discussion on feasibility, risk, timing

### Hour 4: Decision
- Master Coordinator summarizes consensus
- Mentor gives final wisdom
- You choose #1 opportunity
- Begin product spec writing

## What Gets Decided

By end of meeting:

1. **#1 Opportunity:** Clear choice
2. **Why It Wins:** 3-5 key factors
3. **Starting Point:** MVP spec outline
4. **Timeline:** 2-week MVP plan
5. **Success Metrics:** How we measure success
6. **Risk Mitigation:** Top 3 risks + responses
