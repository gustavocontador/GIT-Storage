## Response Guidelines (for agents)

### Tone
- Professional, grateful, never defensive
- "We thank the reviewer for this insightful comment"
- "We appreciate this important observation"

### Structure per response
1. **Acknowledge** the reviewer's point
2. **Explain** what action was taken (and why)
3. **Point** to the specific change in the manuscript

### When you disagree
- "We respectfully disagree because [evidence/reasoning]"
- Always provide evidence for your position
- Offer a compromise when possible

### Common responses
- **Agreed:** "We agree with this suggestion and have revised accordingly..."
- **Partially agreed:** "We agree with the overall point. However, we have opted to... because..."
- **Respectfully declined:** "We appreciate this suggestion. However, we believe the current approach is appropriate because..."
