# Task: Squad Kickoff

**ID:** squad-kickoff
**Tier:** All agents
**Duration:** 4 hours
**Elicit:** true

## Objective

Activate the AI Empire Squad, align all 10 agents on the AI-FIRST project vision, establish communication patterns, and prepare for opportunity discovery.

## What Happens

All 10 agents activate and align on:
- Project vision & success criteria
- Team roles & responsibilities
- Communication patterns & cadence
- First steps & timeline

## Prerequisites

- Project name: AI-FIRST ✓
- Squad location: ./squads/ai-empire-squad/ ✓
- All 10 agents defined ✓

## Elicitation Points

Answer these questions to customize for your situation:

### 1. What's Your Vision?
```
Describe your high-level vision for AI-FIRST:
- What problem are you solving?
- Who would use it?
- Why does it matter?

Example: "Build AI-native tools for solo entrepreneurs
         to manage their entire business autonomously"
```

### 2. What's Your Timeline?
```
What's your urgency?
- [ ] Slow: Test ideas, learn (3-6 months)
- [ ] Medium: Build MVP and validate (6-12 weeks)
- [ ] Fast: Ship product ASAP (2-4 weeks)
- [ ] Extreme: Already have customers waiting (days)
```

### 3. Your Background/Expertise
```
What do you bring to the table?
- [ ] Technical (coding/architecture)
- [ ] Business (sales/marketing)
- [ ] Product (design/UX)
- [ ] Generalist (bit of everything)
- [ ] Learner (new to this)
```

### 4. Success Definition
```
What does "million-dollar business" mean to you?
- [ ] $1M annual revenue
- [ ] $1M in lifetime profit
- [ ] $1M valuation
- [ ] $1M in personal income
- [ ] Impact above all else
```

### 5. Key Constraints
```
What are your hard constraints?
- Time available per week? (hours)
- Budget to invest? ($)
- Technology preferences?
- Market focus? (B2B, B2C, Creator economy, etc.)
```

## Squad Responsibilities

### Master Coordinator
- [ ] Welcome team and set tone
- [ ] Document vision & success criteria
- [ ] Create communication channels
- [ ] Schedule weekly roundtable meetings

### Tier 2 (Roundtable)
- [ ] AI Strategist: "How can Claude Code + AIOS win here?"
- [ ] Business Strategist: "What business model could work?"
- [ ] Product Manager: "What would delight users?"
- [ ] Each prepares perspective for opportunity discovery

### Tier 3 (Builders)
- [ ] Systems Architect: "What tech stack will we use?"
- [ ] Developer Lead: "What's our quality standard?"
- [ ] Data Specialist: "What metrics will we track?"
- [ ] DevOps Engineer: "Where will we deploy?"

### Tier 4 (Guardians)
- [ ] Mentor: "What should this founder know?"
- [ ] Compliance: "What legal/security baseline?"
- [ ] Both prepare for ongoing oversight
