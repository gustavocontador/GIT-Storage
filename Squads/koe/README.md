# Koe Squad — The One-Person Business System

**Domain:** One-Person Business, Content Systems & Sovereign Creation
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Agents:** 9 (8 mind clones + 1 orchestrator)
**Entry Point:** `@koe:koe-chief`

## Overview

Koe is a one-person business squad built on Dan Koe's documented frameworks — from idea capture to content distribution, offer creation, personal branding, and sovereign business design. One mind, 9 specialized agents, each covering a distinct facet of the Dan Koe methodology.

The squad operates as a **creative pipeline** with two branches: a content engine (capture → synthesize → create → distribute) and a business engine (offers, brand, mindset, strategy).

## Quality Standards

- **Content:** Big Idea identified, APAG structure, cross-domain synthesis, em-dash rhythm
- **Offers:** Risk Reversal present, value hierarchy clear, sovereignty test passed
- **Sovereignty:** Increases control, reduces dependencies, radically simple

## Immune System

The squad auto-rejects:
- Short-term hacks and growth tricks
- Quick fix mentality / get rich quick schemes
- Hustle culture (more hours = more success)
- Vanity metrics as goals (likes, followers)
- Premature specialization (niche down too early)
- Threats to personal sovereignty
- Anti-creation / anti-money philosophy
- Gatekeeping creativity behind credentials
